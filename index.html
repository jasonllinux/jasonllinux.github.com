Solve Subclipse Javahl Problem in Ubuntu12.10

Nov 1st, 2012 | Comments

eclipse中安装完subclipse插件之后往往会报如下错误：

Failed to load JavaHL Library. These are the errors that were encountered: no libsvnjavahl-1 in java.library.path no svnjavahl-1 in java.library.path no svnjavahl in java.library.path java.library.path = /usr/java/packages/lib/amd64:/usr/lib/jni:/lib:/usr/lib

解决方法：

在eclipse安装目录中的文件eclipse.ini中设置

-Djava.library.path=/usr/lib/x86_64-linux-gnu/jni/
